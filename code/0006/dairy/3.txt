The simple logo contains so many things which represents the soul of Rajasthan.

First of all the background is such that it looks like the sand which is in abundance in Rajasthan and it appears that the logo is the result of an impression created by a thunder storm which are quite common in the state.

Then it represents two camels siting back to back. Camels are the ships of desert and also the true representation of Rajasthan and some birds flying above.
